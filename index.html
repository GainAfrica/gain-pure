ABOUT US

WHO WE ARE

We are a non-profit organization dedicated to making a positive impact in Africa. Our mission is to ensure equitable access to high-quality education for individuals from diverse backgrounds and circumstances, regardless of their financial situation. Through a series of customized projects and targeted initiatives, we aim to transform individuals and empower them to create a better future for themselves and their communities. By providing educational opportunities and resources, we believe in breaking the cycle of poverty and fostering sustainable development. Together, we can make a difference and contribute to the growth and progress of Africa.

Learn more

MISSION

“Our mission is to empower and enrich the lives of individuals through education. We aim to cultivate a society where every person has equal access to quality education, regardless of their background or circumstances. By investing in education, we strive to unlock the full potential of individuals, equipping them with the necessary knowledge, skills, and opportunities to thrive in a rapidly changing world. Through collaboration with partners and communities, we are committed to promoting educational equity, fostering lifelong learning, and creating pathways for success for all. Together, we believe in the transformative power of education to create a brighter future for individuals and society as a whole.”

Temitope Fashakin

Founder, GAIN Africa

THE UMBRELLA

LEAP in me which stands for Leadership Entrepreneurship Academic Program: Inspire, Nurture, Mentor, Empower.

The aim is to build up future leaders in Nigeria by creating a culture that is corruption free. This would be made possible by getting to the root of the problem which is working on the younger generation. We would be working with young ones within the age of 7 to 25 in primary, secondary and tertiary schools. They would be prepared for leadership as we build up for them a biography of successful leaders; learning their values and inculcating them. Academics would not be left out as individual weaknesses would be addressed.

Enterprising Animal Thrift

Enterprising Animal Thrift is a business model that generates revenue through investment in livestock animals. The business is innovative in the sense that all stakeholders ( investors) are rewarded without recourse to their involvement in the business operations and economically empowers (training and income) the bottom of the pyramid people involves in the running and operation of the business.

Testimonial

“I am Osionela Josephine OGIOGWA, a native of the Afemai tribe in Edo State, Nigeria. I come from a family of 2, myself being the first born. After high school, I enrolled in the Cambridge Advanced Level program for a year.

I came in contact with Mr Temitope Fashakin in 2022. Knowing him has been a blessing to my academic life. He took an interest in me cause I had good grades in high school and the A Levels program; he believed that I have what it takes to go to school in the United States of America. (I had 3A*s in the October/November 2021 Cambridge Advanced Level examination in Biology, Chemistry and Physics respectively).

The process all started under Qpat Solutions Limited, a company founded by Mr Temitope Fashakin under the umbrella organization,GAIN(Giving Aid International Network), a nongovernmental organization that invests in the academic life of young students in high school and university. This program took care of my SAT by paying for the test and also by tutoring me for the test. I had a total of 1410 at the end of the test.

During my time with Qpat solutions,I was assisted with my application to six schools. I had English study sessions with excellent English Language teachers who helped to make my college essay perfect and presentable. At the end of it all, I gained admission to three schools with scholarships to two. Without the help of this organization, I wouldn't have been able to fulfill my dream of studying in the United States as a lot of steps require guidance.

Words can't explain my feelings of gratitude for the academic help rendered to me through Qpat solutions, the financial help from GAIN and the mentoring help from Mr. Temitope Fashakin and his crew. God bless you all. It has been an amazing journey so far and I look forward to what the Lord has in store for me.”

Osionela Ogiogwa
